Eggle

Description

This is a very easy recipe that you can whip of for a hearty boost of energy in the morning!

Ingredients

  • bagel
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese

Steps

  • Start the bagel in the toaster
  • Start heating a pan to fry your eggs
  • After a minute or two crack the eggs into the pan
  • When the toaster is done spread a good amount of cream cheese onto each open face of the bagel
  • Finally when your eggs are cook through seperate them and place each one onto one of teh halves of the bagel
